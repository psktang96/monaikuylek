Our Story

Who We Are

Gulf Gardens is a Thai-based company founded by Mr. Ittipan Salam and Mr. Atteya Abdildayem, who have over 11 years of experience in exporting. During this time, we have shipped a wide range of plants from the finest nurseries in Thailand to the Middle East and other countries.

Our company is dedicated to providing quality choices for individuals looking for plants and garden supplies, as well as serving contractors who need a reliable source of products. Since the company's founders are Thai and Middle Eastern, they are aware of both the variety of Thai plants and worldwide market needs.
