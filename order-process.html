How to Order

Simple 4-step process to get Thai plants delivered to your door

Step by Step

Our Order Process

We make importing plants from Thailand as smooth as possible. Here's exactly how it works from first contact to delivery.

1

📋 Inquiry & Plant List

Send us your list of desired plants with quantities, sizes, and your destination country. You can reach us via email or WhatsApp. If you're unsure about which plants suit your climate, our team is happy to advise.

📧 Email: info@gulfgardens.co.th  |  📞 +66-989857999
2

💰 Receive Your Quotation

We'll send you a detailed quotation including plant prices, packaging costs, shipping fees, and expected delivery dates. We're transparent about all costs — no hidden fees.

✅ Quotation includes: Plant cost · Packaging · Phytosanitary certificate · Shipping estimate
3

✅ Confirm & Payment

Once you approve the quotation, we confirm the order and begin preparation. A deposit is typically required to begin the process. We work with your timeline and accommodate phased orders.

💳 Payment Methods

🏦 Bank Transfer

Account Name: Gulf Gardens Co., Ltd.
Wire transfer to our Thai bank account.

💱 International Wire

We accept international wire transfers in USD, EUR, and other major currencies.

4

📦 Packing & Quality Check

Our team carefully selects, inspects, and packages each plant using specific handling policies to ensure minimum damage. Every shipment undergoes a strict quality control check before packing.

🌿 All plants are inspected for health, pests, and size conformity before packing
5

🚢 Shipping & Phytosanitary Certificate

We handle all export documentation including the Phytosanitary Certificate required for international plant shipments. Plants are shipped via sea or air freight depending on quantity and urgency.

📄 Documents included: Phytosanitary Certificate · Commercial Invoice · Packing List · Bill of Lading
